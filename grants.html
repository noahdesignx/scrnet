Eligibility

What Makes Me Qualify for a Grant?



In order to be eligible for a grant, you must be diagnosed with a form of gastric cancer (stomach cancer) or the caregiver of the person with the gastric cancer. Grant amounts are based on the funds we have available at the time you apply and are a one-time only gift to use for whatever financial need you deem necessary.
