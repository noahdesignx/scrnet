About Us

Who We Are

In Honor & Loving Memory of Yanira Mitchell, who passed away from Stomach Cancer on September 6, 2008

"We are a 100% volunteer non-profit organization carrying out our mission to assist as many people who qualify for a grant as possible. We know that financial burdens can take a heavy toll on the sick, and receiving any kind of relief is appreciated. We try to stretch funds as far as possible to help those suffering from this deadly disease and to support further research financially. Yanira believed in paying it forward and wanted to help others who are suffering with stomach cancer. For all the lives and people she touched while here on earth, she was truly an angel, hero, and inspiration for us all." - Marcus Mitchell
